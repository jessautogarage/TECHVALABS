Hello! I'm Jesrel Agang, your full-spectrum Tech Partner.

I’m a passionate and results-driven Full Stack Web Developer with over 8 years of experience in the IT industry, including 3 years dedicated to web development. I specialize in helping businesses grow through custom systems, automation, and digital solutions that scale.

My development journey includes building and customizing platforms using technologies like: WordPress, Shopify, PHP, CodeIgniter (MVC), Bootstrap, JavaScript, CSS, Tailwind, Flutter, Node.js, and more. I can confidently work as a: Web Developer, Software Engineer, SEO Specialist, Virtual Assistant, Customer Service Agent, Technical Support Specialist, IT Service Desk Analyst, Cloud Engineer, App Developer, Automation Specialist, and CRM Workflow Integrator.
